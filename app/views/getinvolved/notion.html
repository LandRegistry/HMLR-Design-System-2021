Notion

The Experience Library Team is currently in the process of obtaining licenses for Notion.

The purpose of Notion is:

  • To encourage important research and findings to be shared across the community by facilitating greater transparency and communication between project teams.
  • To store documentation around the user research and design decisions behind experimental components and patterns.
  • To allow the whole community to be involved in the evolution and enhancement of the Experience Library.

Until the Notion licenses are approved and distributed, teams are encouraged to gather documentation around experimental components and patterns in Google Docs.
