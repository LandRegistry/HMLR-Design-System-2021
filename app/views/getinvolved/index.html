Steering group

The HM Land Registry Experience library steering group meets once a month to discuss ways to develop and improve the library.

The steering group also helps support new design proposals, and encourage the communities to get involved and contribute.

It is a cross-functional team made up of interaction and content design, user research, accessibility, and front-end development.

You can contact the steering group on our #design-content slack channel or by emailing lauren.dowling@landregistry.gov.uk.
