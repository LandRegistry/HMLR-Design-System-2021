Suggest new ideas

Anyone working at HM Land Registry can suggest adding a new component or pattern to the Experience Library.

1. Check the backlog

Check if the pattern or component is already on the backlog. If it is, tell the Experience Library team if you want to contribute to it by emailing Lauren Dowling.

2. Share the work

Talk about the pattern or component to the Experience Library team. Share your work via email with Lauren Dowling. It is important for the Experience Library team to know if there are a number of teams working on similar patterns or components.
