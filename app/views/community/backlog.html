Backlog

The Experience Library has an open backlog. Anyone can propose, develop or contribute to new patterns and components, or improvements to existing ones.

The best place to discuss patterns and components is on Slack or you can email Lauren Dowling.

Here is a list of the components, patterns and updates currently on the HMLR Experience Library backlog. They can be:

  • Proposed - someone has suggested a new component or pattern
  • To do - the proposed component or pattern has been agreed and is ready to work on
  • In progress - someone is actively working on the component or pattern

Please note this list is not in order of priority.
