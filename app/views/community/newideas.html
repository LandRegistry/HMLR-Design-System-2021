Contribute

Suggest new ideas

Anyone working at HM Land Registry can suggest adding a new component or pattern to the Experience Library.

1. Check the backlog

Check if the pattern or component is already on the backlog.

2. Contact the Experience Library team

If the pattern or component is already in the backlog and you would like to contribute to its development, please contact the Experience Library team by emailing Lauren Dowling. You will then be added to the working group for this component or pattern.

If the pattern or component does not exist in the backlog, please contact the Experience Library team by emailing Lauren Dowling. Include details of the new component or pattern in your email to provide context on how the contribution criteria is met.

3. Fill in documentation template

Once decided by the steering group that your suggestion does fulfil the criteria, you will be sent the documentation template. This should be filled in alongside any other team members wishing to contribute.
