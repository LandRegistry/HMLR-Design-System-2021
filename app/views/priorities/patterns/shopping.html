E-Commerce shopping experience

What is this pattern?

This pattern mimics an e-commerce experience by allowing users to add the documents they wish to purchase to a shopping bag.

Examples

  • Example

Why was this pattern needed?

Selecting documents for purchase is a transactional process. The team wished to create a sense of familiarity by replicating an on page shopping basket, as well as a checkout page.
