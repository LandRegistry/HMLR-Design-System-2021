Prioritising experimental components and patterns

A few weeks ago, we asked each team to provide a brief summary of the experimental components and patterns currently being explored for use across their service.

Before components or patterns can be added to the Design System, they must first be reviewed by the Design System steering group. Input from the community is needed to help us prioritise which components and patterns to review first.

The task:

  • Look through each experimental component and pattern.
  • Complete the survey.
